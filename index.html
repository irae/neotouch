    • Flip through your content, no matter the screen size.

    NeoTouch shows web content through many devices in a cheaper and effective way, using YQL and Treesaver.js (an open-source HTML5 & JavaScript framework).

    If you're not that nerdy: no matter the size of your screen, or how you interact with it (clicking, touching or pressing buttons), you'll always have a great experience, as if you're flipping the pages of a magazine.

    When you activate NeoTouch, our little suricates selects the relevant content of the article you're reading and convert it for an amazing interface, that looks and feels like a magazine.

    This way, you can navigate through the content as you already knows how to do with an regular magazine: by flipping it's pages.
