    • Flip through your content, no matter the screen size.

    When you activate NeoTouch, our little suricates select the relevant content within the article you're reading and convert it into an amazing interface that looks and feels like a magazine.

    No matter the size of your screen, or how you interact with it (clicking, touching or pressing buttons), you'll always have a great experience.

    This way, you can navigate through the content the same way you do with a regular magazine: by flipping its pages.

    NeoTouch is easy to apply to your content in a low cost effective way. Since it's Open Source Software, you can grab a copy of the code and install it you your server.

    It's a metter of creating an YQL table, an Yahoo! Technology that makes any content accecible programatically, and changing a few configurations.

    Then, NeoTouch uses Treesaver.js, an open-source HTML5 & JavaScript framework, to automatically present your content adaptively on any device, making it fell like a magazine.

    G1, by Globo, is one of the biggest content providers in Brazil. We used NeoTouch to collect and present G1's content within NeoTouch.
