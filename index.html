    • Flip through your content, no matter the screen size.

    When you activate NeoTouch, our little suricates select the relevant content within the article you're reading and convert it into an amazing interface that looks and feels like a magazine.

    No matter the size of your screen, or how you interact with it (clicking, touching or pressing buttons), you'll always have a great experience.

    This way, you can navigate through the content the same way you do with a regular magazine: by flipping its pages.

    NeoTouch is easy to apply to your content in a cost effective way. Since it's Open Source Software, you can grab a copy of the code and install it on your server.

    It's a metter of creating an YQL table, a Yahoo! Technology that makes any content accessible programatically. And then all it takes is to change a few settings.

    Then, NeoTouch uses Treesaver.js, an open-source HTML5 & JavaScript framework, to automatically present your content adaptively on any device, making it feel like a magazine.

    G1, by Globo, is one of the biggest content providers in Brazil. We used YQL to collect and NeoTouch to present G1's "Ciência em Saúde" content section in a magazine like way.
